# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Drivers in Chandler, Mesa, Gilbert, Tempe, and Phoenix who need convenient dent, bumper, paint, or light collision repair at their home or workplace. Visitors are usually evaluating visible damage and want a fast, credible estimate without arranging a body-shop visit.

## Product Purpose

Present CW Autobody Mobile's repair capabilities, establish confidence through clear visual evidence and service terms, and turn qualified visitors into phone calls or photo estimate requests.

## Brand Personality

Direct, mechanical, and dependable. The experience should feel skilled and energetic while keeping the business information practical and easy to verify.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic body-shop templates, stock-photo collages, heavy image overlays that hide repair detail, cropped vehicle imagery, inflated claims, and decorative effects that compete with the work itself.

## Design Principles

1. Show the workmanship and vehicle detail clearly.
2. Make calling or requesting an estimate immediate.
3. Use motion to demonstrate transformation, not as decoration.
4. Keep service claims specific and verifiable.
5. Preserve legibility without obscuring the primary imagery.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard usability. Respect reduced-motion preferences, preserve usable poster imagery when animation is reduced, and keep core contact actions available without relying on scroll effects.
